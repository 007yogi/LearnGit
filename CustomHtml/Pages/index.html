Mastering Botany

Nurturing Your Blossoms

Botanical health requires understanding the harmony between elements. Follow our foundational care rules.

The Lighting Harmony

Most flowering plants crave bright, indirect sunlight to synthesize energy. Avoid direct noon sunlight which can scorch delicate petals and bleach pigments.

Hydration Precision

Overwatering is the number one cause of root decay. Water deeply but allow the top two inches of soil to completely dry out before hydrating again.

Aerated Nourishment

Invest in well-draining, nutrient-rich soil blends. Mix potting soil with perlite and peat moss to allow roots to breathe and thrive without dampness.
