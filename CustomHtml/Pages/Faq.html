Frequently Asked Questions

What is Flora Aura?

Flora Aura is a curated showcase of rare and beautiful flowers, offering care guides and botanical information.

How can I purchase a flower?

Currently, we provide information only. Purchasing options will be added in future releases.

Where do you source your flowers?

Our collection is sourced from partner growers worldwide, focusing on sustainability and rarity.

Can I submit my own flower for inclusion?

Yes! Reach out via the Contact page with photos and details, and we’ll consider featuring it.
